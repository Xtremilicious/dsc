DevExpo

Google Crowdsource in association with DSC KiiT is going to organize a full day event 'DevExpo19' in KiiT DU on April 7th. The expo will revolve around students, techies, designers, brainstormers and anyone who wants to increase their knowledge in these domains. The event will showcase some great sessions on some of the burning topics in the industry like Actions on Google, Machine Learning, Design Thinking and many more. There will be exciting networking sessions for the 'introvert' in you. The event will be power-packed with some amazing and enthusiastic people, and it'll be regret to miss it.
